StudyConnect

Collaborative Study and Learning Platform

Overview

StudyConnect is a comprehensive collaborative learning platform that brings students together to study smarter, not harder. By combining virtual study rooms, resource sharing, peer tutoring, and intelligent study tools, StudyConnect creates a vibrant learning community where students can achieve academic success together.

Key Features

  • Virtual study rooms with video, audio, and screen sharing
  • Collaborative document editing and note-taking
  • Peer tutoring marketplace connecting students and tutors
  • Study group formation based on courses and interests
  • Shared resource library with file organization
  • Calendar integration for study sessions and deadlines
  • AI-powered study recommendations and scheduling
  • Gamification with achievements and leaderboards
  • Discussion forums and Q&A communities
  • Progress tracking and goal setting tools

Technology Stack

Next.js React WebRTC Socket.io PostgreSQL Redis AWS Stripe Docker GraphQL

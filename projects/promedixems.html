ProMedixEMS

Comprehensive EMS and Medical Training Platform

Overview

ProMedixEMS is a cutting-edge Emergency Medical Services training platform designed to provide comprehensive education and certification preparation for EMS professionals. The platform combines interactive learning modules, real-world scenarios, and advanced assessment tools to deliver an unparalleled training experience.

Key Features

  • Interactive medical protocols and treatment algorithms
  • Virtual patient scenarios with realistic decision-making
  • Comprehensive drug reference database with dosage calculators
  • NREMT exam preparation with practice tests
  • Video tutorials from experienced paramedics and physicians
  • Progress tracking and performance analytics
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design for on-the-go learning
  • Continuing education credit tracking
  • Team collaboration tools for training groups
  • Offline mode for studying without internet connection

Technology Stack

React Node.js Express MongoDB PostgreSQL AWS Redux TypeScript WebRTC Socket.io
